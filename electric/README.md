# Electric guitar samples (Clean preset)

Used by the `clean` and `overdrive` tone presets. Filename convention:
`eN-FF.mp3` where `N` = string number (1=high E, 6=low E) and `FF` = fret
number (00–24).

## Pitch coverage

MIDI 40 (E2, low E open) through MIDI 86 (D6). Pitches above MIDI 86 (top
2 frets of the high-E string) are pitch-shifted by Tone.Sampler from the
nearest available pitch.
